American Goldfinch

A bright yellow flash of beauty, August 21, 2021

The American Goldfinch is one of the most distinctive birds in all of the world. With is small stature, some might not notice it hiding in a bush or a tree - but when it's out for all to see, it is quite the sight to behold. Being the state bird of 3 different states, it is a commonly found one - with 24 million around right now, it is a bird you could see in your backyard.

Red Cardinal

The State Bird of Virginia August 22, 2021

The red cardinal is commonly found in the state of Virginia. Known for its vibrant red color, this bird darts through the air at speeds of up to (____). While red is the most prominent color on this bird, its black chin and face area make it even more unique. The females, on the other hand, have a distinctive brown undercoating. This bird is found in all seasons of the year, and when it is snowy, it makes for a sight to behold, as you spot a flash of red perching on a brown branch with lush snow all around.
